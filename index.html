D1E1 - Introductions / Show & Tell

Hi, I’m Swetha, a 19 year old, I love doing watercolors and animations. I am from Pondicherry and my hometown is Tamil Nadu. I love mixing traditional art forms with contemporary design techniques. Whether I’m capturing tiny watercolors or animated characters, I find joy in every stage of the creative process.
